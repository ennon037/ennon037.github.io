项目构想与确定
Project conception and determination

我确定了项目为线下桌面游戏。
在此之前，我曾想要采用线上游戏的形式，经过对于技术等一系列问题的考虑，我最终选择了线下游戏。
随后，我开始制定游戏规则。

I identified the project as an offline board game.
Before that, I wanted to adopt the form of online games. After considering a series of issues such as technology, I finally chose offline games.
Then I began to make the rules of the game.

Practice and production: rules and instructions of the game

这是一个游戏规则的雏形，我还只是把它记在word文档里。直到26日才算出现了一个较完整的版本。
在游戏规则设计进行到一半时，我已经有在制作一些能简化游戏规则的示意图了。但是这些示意图并不清晰易懂，甚至是有些混乱。在这些示意图的基础上，我进行改进依次制作了三个版本的说明书。在首页的”草图设计“一栏，我发布了说明书的几个版本。

A more complete version did not appear until the 26th.

But the diagrams are not clear and easy to understand, even confusing.
On the basis of these sketches, I improved and produced three versions of the manual successively.
In the "sketch design" section of the front page, I posted several versions of the manual.

测试与改进
Testing and improvement

我觉得这是切实体现玩家权力的时刻，测试的目的也就是为了服务玩家体验感。
测试阶段可以说是制作阶段的一部分:除却初期构建的，说明书和规则的改进是与测试同步进行的。我将在下面写下这些测试对卡牌、规则和说明书的改进的作用。

I think this is a real power moment, and the purpose of testing is to serve the player experience.
The test phase is arguably part of the build phase: aside from the initial build, specification and rule improvements are synchronized with the test.
Below, I'll write about how these tests have improved the CARDS, rules, and specifications.

测试于卡牌
About the cards

在第一次测试中，两幅卡牌的弊端就暴露出来了：过于繁琐，不但难以操作，还容易出现各种没有记载到的bug。考虑到更改卡牌样式比更改整个规则更加方便，我在四色卡牌和数字牌组合的基础上进行了改造，舍弃数字牌，将卡牌类型的区分安排成颜色的不同。 

In the first test, the drawbacks of the two CARDS were exposed: they were too complicated, difficult to operate, and prone to various unrecorded bugs.
Considering that it is more convenient to change the card style than to change the whole rule, I made a transformation based on the combination of four color CARDS and number CARDS, discarding the number CARDS and arranging the card types into different colors.

测试于规则
About the rules

在测试中，我担任厂商的角色，三位朋友是“消费者”。所幸我的朋友们都是一些非常熟悉桌面游戏的人，从最开始游戏进行的磕磕绊绊，到后来能顺利进行，也是这些朋友帮助我改进规则的结果。

In the test, I played the role of manufacturer, and three friends were "consumers."
Fortunately, my friends are people who are very familiar with board games, and they helped me to improve the rules of the game from the beginning to the end.

测试于说明书
About the The instructions
在最后一次测试中，我找家人帮助我进行了测试。不熟悉此类桌面游戏的测试者加入， 考验着规则展示方式的清晰度。
我在说明方式上进行了四次改进：
①从word文档转为单张图片
②将中英双语的说明书改为分别写有英语和中文
③对说明书进行简化
④将说明书（主要用于讲述规则）与词语解释分开，制成可撕开的一张卡片

In the last test, I asked my family to help me with the test.
Testers unfamiliar with such board games joined in, testing the clarity of how the rules were presented.
I have made four improvements in the way of illustration:
From Word document to single picture
(2) The bilingual instruction is changed into English and Chinese respectively
(3) to simplify the specification
(4) Separate the instruction (mainly used to tell the rules) from the word explanation, and make a card that can be torn apart
