草图设计

The sketch design

这个页面用于展示我关于卡牌和说明书的设计草图和相关的改进过程。
This page is used to show my design sketches of CARDS and instructions and the related improvement process.

  • All
这是我最初的设想，卡牌是不同类型的两幅，一副是数字牌，另一幅是颜色牌，两幅牌合在一起才能表示游戏中商品的价值。
This is my initial idea. There are two CARDS of different types, one is a number card and the other is a color card. Only when the two CARDS are combined together can they represent the value of goods in the game.
我发觉两幅牌非常地不方便，就将它们合成既有数字又有颜色，用颜色区分类型、数字控制大小的一副牌。
I found the two CARDS very inconvenient, so I combined them into a deck with both Numbers and colors, using colors to distinguish the type and size of the CARDS.
这是我更改卡牌类型后的初稿。中间的图片是我的logo，现在看来的确过于粗制滥造……
This is the first draft after I changed the card type.
The image in the middle is my logo, which now seems too crude...
这是我的卡牌设计终稿。基于上次的呈现效果简陋，我绘制了概念图作为装饰。】
This is the final draft of my card design.

第一版的说明书是对word文档上零散规则的集合。这一版说明书从逻辑方面上看很有问题。
The first version of the specification is a collection of loose rules on Word documents.
This edition of the specification is logically questionable.
第二版说明书基于word文档和上一版说明书，将玩家介绍、卡牌介绍、规则流程和相关解释划成几个部分，变成三联。
The second version of the manual is based on the Word document and the previous version of the manual, which divides the player introduction, card introduction, rule flow and related explanation into several parts and forms a triple.
第三版更改了卡牌设计和部分规则。
The third edition changed the card design and some of the rules.
第四版对于整个规则进行了简化，并将相关解释和规则说明变为可撕的两张。
The fourth edition simplifies the entire rule and changes the relevant interpretation and rule description into two pieces that can be torn.
